5 places I want to go after MCO

George Town

George Town is the colorful, multicultural capital of the Malaysian island of Penang.

Langkawi

Langkawi, officially known as Langkawi, the Jewel of Kedah, is a district and an archipelago of 99 islands in the Malacca Strait.

Petronas Twin Towers

The Petronas Towers, or the Petronas Twin Towers, are twin skyscrapers in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

Tokyo Skytree

Tokyo Skytree is a broadcasting and observation tower in Sumida, Tokyo.

N Seoul Tower

N Seoul Tower is a communication and observation tower located on Namsan Mountain in central Seoul, South Korea.
